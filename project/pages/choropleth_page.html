Analysing network relay activity Across U.S. States


Welcome to the Choropleth map visualization by SilkViser! This interactive map showcases the geographical distribution of blockchain relay nodes throughout the United States, with a unique focus on the frequency of relaying activities by individual IP addresses. States are color-coded to represent the number of times IP addresses within those states have relayed blockchain data. Darker shades indicate a higher frequency of relaying activity, highlighting areas with more active blockchain participation.

  • Knowing the distribution and frequency of relay nodes helps in assessing the robustness and security of a blockchain network. Areas with dense relay activity might be more resilient to attacks or failures since the presence of multiple nodes can ensure the network stays operational even if some nodes fail.

  • Beyond textual and tabular data, this visual representation offers an intuitive way for users to explore and comprehend network activity.
